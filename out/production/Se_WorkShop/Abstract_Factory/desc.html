Abstract Factory is a creational design pattern, which solves the problem of creating entire product families without specifying their concrete classes. Abstract Factory defines an interface for creating all distinct products ,
but leaves the actual product creation to concrete factory classes.

Provide the interface : creating families of related or dependent objets without specifying their concrete classes

super factory that create other factory, factory of factories

create of objects via abstraction

provide the encapsulation mechanism to factories : factory method is the subset o this pattern



but there is often a concrete class to implemented for each family

when to use this:

when a system should be independent, how the products are created and composed and represented

when we deal with multiple fact

when the (problem) domain has different families of objects present and each family used under different circumstances

when a family of related objects designed to used together

when you provide class lib and reveal their interfaces not their implementations
